THE APP

What? Hello! Curious about the supposedly ideal weight of someone your sex and height? Then, this app is for you. The app uses four different formulas, namely by Hamwi (1964), Devine (1974), Robinson (1983), and Miller (1983), in solving you ideal weight. The app calculates so fast so there is no need to go search up the formula and manually solve it. Unfortunately, we are sad to say that this app is only for accurate adults whose height is 5 feet and above. It isn’t accurate for kids and pregnant women.

So, how does it work?

Step 1: Indicate what your sex and height is.

It is important to know your sex and height because the formulas are different for each sex and will depend on your height. In this app, we only take height values in feet and inches. Take note that height values shouldn’t be negative or the inches value shouldn’t be greater than or equal to 12. Please convert 12 inches to 1 foot.

Step 2: Press Solve.

After inputting your sex and height, simply press the green “Solve” button and the app will automatically compute your weight.

Step 3: Tada! You can see what your ideal weight should be.

The app will indicate four values on what your ideal weight is depending on which formula was used. This is summarized at the bottom of the calculator, just below solve button.
